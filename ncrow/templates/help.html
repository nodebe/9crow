Popular Topics

These are the most frequently asked questions about 9crow.

I forgot the password for my account.
Go to the Login page and click on forgot password. You will be redirected to a page where you fill in your email address and a new password with which you can login will be sent to your email.
How do I withdraw funds from my account?
On your dashboard, click on the withdraw link. You will be redirected to a page where you fill your details and click on Continue. Note that you have to have an existing bank account in your profile.
How do I link bank account to my account?
On your dashboard, below the main menu, there is a secondary menu that has a link "Bank Accounts". Click on it and fill in your account details. Note that you can only have two accounts in a profile.
How do I receive payments?
You recieve a payment when the both parties involved in a transaction have marked the transaction as fulfilled.
Where is my refund?
Refunds are made directly to your bank account if a dispute is settled in your favour. Note that you have to link an active bank account to your profile.
How Can I View My Payments History?
Payment history can be viewed on your dashboard. The vendor activities shows your transactions as a seller and the user activities shows your transactions as a buyer.
How do I request payments or send an invoice?
Click on the Recieve link on the main menu. Fill in the transaction details and click on Continue.
I didn't get the right products?
Navigate to the transaction through your dashboard and raise a dispute with the pictures as evidence.
